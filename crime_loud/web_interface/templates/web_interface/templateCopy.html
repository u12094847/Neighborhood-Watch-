What is Crime_loud?

Crime_loud is a web application that allows to upload information in the form of images, audio, and video that might be used by law enforcement.

Upload images

Upload images of any suspicious behaviour, criminal offence or wrong doings. This will help law enforcement in dealing with cases.

Upload videos

Upload videos of anysuspicious behaviour, criminal offence or wrong doings. Videos are a powerfull tool in solving criminal and other cases.

Upload audio clips

Upload audio clips or recordings of anysuspicious behaviour, criminal offence or wrong doings. Audio recordings can lead to the solving of crimes and or assisting in investigations by forensic specialists.

Assist law enforcement

By uploading content to the site, you are helping law enforcement and also helping your community in making a better place by putting away unjust individuals.
